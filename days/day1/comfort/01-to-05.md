## 1.  Why is this happening to me?

**Category:** Spiritual Churn & Purpose JUSTBR
**Keywords:** Victimhood, Unfairness, Meaning, Despair, Cosmic Plan JUSTBR
**Description of Hurt:** A crushing feeling that the universe is cruel, indifferent, or punishing you personally, leaving you feeling helpless and filled with resentment.

**5 Perspectives Offering Comfort:**

1.  In the Dwapara Age of energy, intense friction is often the acceleration of light breaking through old, dense inner structures.
2.  This turbulence is not a punishment; it is a profound spiritual churn signaling that your soul is ready to expand.
3.  The Cosmic Mother does not cause you pain out of malice; she allows the chisel to work so your true divine spark can be revealed.
4.  Shifting from "Why is this happening _to_ me?" to "What is this awakening _in_ me?" restores your inner sovereignty.
5.  The heaviest ruts are often the exact soil required to grow the wisdom that will eventually become your life's greatest legacy.

4.  You are experiencing a sacred re-ordering of your consciousness, not a permanent structural collapse.
5.  Your value is inherent to your being, long wrought by the Creator, and is never dependent on your psychological productivity.

## 5.  Why have I lost all joy in the things I used to love?

**Category:** Ebbs and Flows JUSTBR
**Keywords:** Flatness, Apathy, Anhedonia, Colourless, Fatigue JUSTBR
**Description of Hurt:** A gray, lifeless state of mind where the world has lost its color, and even your favorite passions feel meaningless and empty.

**5 Perspectives Offering Comfort:**

1.  Life is a giant merry-go-round; hitting a flat rut is a natural part of the cyclical rhythm of existence.
2.  Your system is experiencing a necessary spiritual winter—the soil must rest before the seeds of the next flow can sprout.
3.  Do not force an artificial enthusiasm; accept the colorlessness as a sign to turn inward and practice gentle stillness.
4.  As sure as bad comes your way, so shall good grace your life anew; the sunlight has not vanished, it is just around the bend.
5.  This low phase is focusing your character, building a deep well of empathy that will eventually comfort others in their winter.
