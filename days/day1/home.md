Here is a clear, high-level breakdown of the current **Vishwas website**, exploring its core sections, philosophy, and layout.

## Core Philosophy

The site positions Vishwas as a non-judgmental space bridging severe mental health challenges with deep spiritual exploration. A central tagline beautifully captures this mission:

> _"The waters in which a mystic swims in are the same waters in which a person with so-called mental illness drowns in. Our attempt is to help them at least float in these waters."_
